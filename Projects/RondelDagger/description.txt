Rondel Dagger
---
The focus on this project was to further improve my texturing primarily with beaten metals. I found that working on metal textures can be hard to look natural and add details, part of this is due to managing both metallic and roughness values which can occasionally cause conflicts. Overall I was pretty happy with the dagger and decided to make a scene around it. All assets were made by me and revolve around the key item to tell a story.  
---
Blender, Zbrush, Substance Painter,RizomUV, Marmoset Toolbag
